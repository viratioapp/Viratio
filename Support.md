# Viratio Support

Welcome to the support page for **Viratio**.

Viratio is a fitness and workout tracking app designed to help users log workouts and view training data and trends in a simple, user-friendly way.

## Contact

If you need help, have questions, or want to report a bug, please contact:

**Email:** viratio.development@gmail.com

We aim to respond within a few business days.

## Common topics

You can contact support for help with:

- App functionality
- Subscription questions
- Restoring purchases
- Bug reports
- General feedback

## Important

Viratio is a fitness and workout logging app.  
It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
