# Privacy Policy — Viratio

**Last updated:** 4 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Correlation Development handles information when you use Viratio.

1) Summary 
* Your workout data is stored locally on your device.
* We do not require you to create an account.
* We do not sell your data.
* You can export your data as a CSV file from within the App.

2) Information you provide
The App allows you to enter fitness/workout information such as:
* Exercise names, sets, reps, weights, workout notes, dates/times;
* Optional user settings (e.g., units, body weight if you choose to store it).
This information is stored on-device to provide the App’s functionality.

3) Information we collect automatically
We do not collect or store your workout data on our servers as part of normal App operation.
Purchases: If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes the transaction. We receive confirmation of entitlement (whether a subscription is active) via Apple’s StoreKit.

4) Data storage
Workout data and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS storage mechanisms in the app’s private iOS container. We do not host your workout data on our servers.

5) Data sharing
We do not share your workout data with third parties.


6) CSV export
The App allows you to export your workout history as a CSV file. Export is initiated by you, and you control where you save or share that exported file. Once exported, the privacy and security of the file depend on the apps or services you share it with. The App also allows you to import CSV files of the same format. It is recommended to export a CSV file and store it in a chosen location to back up your data. 

7) Analytics, tracking, and advertising
* We do not use advertising trackers.
* We do not sell personal data.
* We do not use third-party analytics SDKs that collect personal data.

8) Your choices and controls
* You can delete workout entries inside the App.
* You can delete all App data by deleting the App from your device.
* You control any exported CSV files.

9) Data security
We take reasonable measures to protect data within the App environment. However, no method of storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and any exported files.

10) Children’s privacy
The App is not intended for users under 18. We do not collect personal data to our servers.


11) International users
If you use the App outside Sweden, your device processes data locally. Purchases are handled by Apple under Apple’s terms and policies.

12) Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

13) Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: viratio.development@gmail.com
